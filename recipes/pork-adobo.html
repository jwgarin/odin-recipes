Pork Adobo

Description

Pork adobo is a popular Filipino dish that was derived from Spanish culture.

An aromatic saucy recipe that balances saltiness and sourness flavor of the ingredients infused with the pork.

It's best complimented with steamed rice on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Pork (cube cut)
  • 5 table spoon of oil
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of vinegar
  • 3 calamansi (juiced)
  • 2 chilis

Steps

  1. Saute the onion, garlic, chili, and pepper in medium heat
  2. Add pork and stir for 3 minutes in medium heat
  3. Add soy sauce , calamansi, and vinegar
  4. Simmer the mixture for 15 minutes in low heat
  5. Stir until the water subsides
  6. Serve and enjoy
