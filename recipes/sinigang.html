Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a popular Filipino cuisine. A pork soup that contains a tamarind twist. It contains various vegetables that compliments the soup's meaty rich and thick sourness flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pork (cube cut)
  • 2 teaspoon of oil
  • 3 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tomato (sliced)
  • 5 pechay leaves
  • 3 okras
  • 1 sinigang mix
  • 5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Saute onion, garlic, and pepper in medium heat
  2. Add pork , salt and stir for 3 minutes in medium heat
  3. Add water and simmer while covered for 30 minutes in low heat
  4. Add pechay leaves, okras, and sinigang mix
  5. Serve and enjoy!
